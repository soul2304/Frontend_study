Akaza

Akaza (Upper Rank Three) is one of the Twelve Kizuki, the strongest demons who serve Muzan Kibutsuji. He is famous for his incredible martial arts skills and terrifying strength. Despite being a villain, he has one of the most emotional and tragic backstories in the series.


Boruto

Boruto Uzumaki is the son of Naruto and the main protagonist of Boruto: Naruto Next Generations. He is a naturally talented ninja who struggles to step out of his father's shadow and find his own identity. With powers like the Karma seal and the Jōgan, Boruto grows into one of the strongest young shinobi of his generation.


Tanjiro

Tanjiro Kamado is the kind-hearted main protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. After his family is killed by demons and his sister Nezuko is turned into one, he becomes a Demon Slayer to find a cure and protect others. Known for his compassion, determination, and Water/ Sun Breathing techniques, Tanjiro grows into one of the strongest and most compassionate warriors in the series.


Sung Jin Woo

Sung Jin-Woo is the main protagonist of Solo Leveling, starting as the weakest hunter in the world before gaining a unique ability that lets him grow infinitely stronger. Through his “System,” he becomes a powerful Shadow Monarch capable of commanding an army of shadows. His journey is one of transformation—from a vulnerable hunter to one of the most powerful beings in the entire universe.
